## Honest framing before you start

- **$2k/month by Oct 5 is a stretch goal, not a guaranteed outcome.** Starting from zero technical background today, the realistic range for combined freelance + bounty income by Oct 5 is closer to $300-800/month, based on how these paths typically play out for beginners. $2k/month puts you in the upper tail of outcomes — possible if you're consistent, land a paying client early, or get a job offer with decent pay. Treat this plan as pointed at the best realistic outcome, not a promise.
- **Weeks 1-6 below are full day-by-day.** Weeks 7-11 are weekly milestones only — I can't responsibly pre-script your Sept 20th task today, since it depends on things that haven't happened yet (interviews landed, bounty progress, client responses). Come back partway through and I'll turn the current week into day-by-day detail based on where you actually are.
- **This is a living document, not a script to follow blindly.** Your own stated method is fail → ask → adjust. If a day's task isn't working, that's information — come back and we'll adjust, not push through on autopilot.
- Hours are calibrated to a 12hr/day baseline, mixing passive learning with active hands-on so you don't burn out on one mode.
